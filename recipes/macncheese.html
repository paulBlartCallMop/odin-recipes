Macaroni & Cheese Recipe

Ingredients

  • Macaroni noodles
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Cheddar & Parmesan cheese
  • Salt & pepper
  • Paprika
  • Bread crumbs

Steps

  1. Boil noodles, drain, then transfer to baking dish
  2. Make cheese sause, pour over noodles and stir
  3. Make the topping, spread it and sprinke with paprika
  4. Bake mac & cheese until the topping is golden brown
