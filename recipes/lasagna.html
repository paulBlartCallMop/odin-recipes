Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients

  • Italian sausage
  • Ground beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce and tomato paste
  • Sugar
  • Parsley
  • Basil
  • Salt
  • Italian seasoning
  • Fennel seeds
  • Black pepper
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheese
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
